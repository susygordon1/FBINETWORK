A covenant community for founders,
entrepreneurs & people in pivot.

There's a specific kind of loneliness that comes with building something. Church feeds your soul. Family loves you. But when you're in the middle of a pivot, bootstrapping a dream, or just trying to make payroll — you need people who get it. The FBI Network is that room. We pray together. We fast together. We hold each other accountable, and show up the next Monday to celebrate what moved.

What Makes FBI Different
Every person in this room is seen, supported, and held to something greater.

We are founders, entrepreneurs, and builders who show up for each other week over week. We bring our faith, our hustle, and our commitment to impact — and we hold each other accountable to the goals we set the week before.

We are individually building our own businesses, but together we are making an impact on our community, our city, and beyond.

What makes us different? Show up Monday and find out.
The Meeting
One Hour. Two Blocks. Every Monday.
Minutes 0–30
The Briefing
  • Faith A scripture, prayer request, or word of encouragement anchoring your week.
  • Business Who you are and what you do. What did you learn this week? Tools, wins, conversations worth sharing.
  • Impact Your one community goal for the week. Specific. Actionable. You report back next Monday.
Minutes 30–60
FBI's Most Wanted
  • The Speaker One subject matter expert. Thirty minutes. Lived experience only — no theory.
  • The Topic A business skill, legal lesson, tool, or insight that changed everything. Real and practical.
  • The Close Every speaker closes with a scripture, then passes the baton to next week's Most Wanted.
  • The Contribution We pass the hat for the FBI cause. Kingdom-minded giving, no pressure.
